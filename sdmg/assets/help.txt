Win: Defeat your opponent(s) by fiercly attacking until they yield or plummet into nothingness.
Lose: Run out of hearts or fall into depths of the abyss.
Heart: This is your heart, don't let your enemies take it from you. Defend it as it is your life energy.
PowerBar: Performing an action costs energy from the powerbar, deplete it and you'll be rendered helpless.
Attacking: You have a short and long-range attack at your disposal, use it at your discretion.
Blocking: A defensive move to protect you from attacks.
Evading: Perform a roll to evade opponents, attacks and projectiles.
